Exoskeleton

My inconsistent mind
Contradicts my heart
Infuriate me
Incinerate me
Compromise my integrity

Cataclysmic ernergies
Penetrate my barriers
Parasites
Bacteriophages
Invade my exoskeleton

They say it's unavoidable
I think I disagree
It might be irrevocable
It seems a little arbitrary
Unvoluntary comedy's
Intrinsic to this metaphor

Ubiquitous cruelty
Seems to defy my altruism
I have to maintain a sense
Of transcendence
To keep my sanity

Misunderstood conformity
Completes the vicious cycle
The failing system still upholds
The remnants of
A sick society


(Autumn 2006)
